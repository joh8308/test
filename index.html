How to install NOD CoCo?

You can install and use NOD CoCo both in your Mobile and PC as well.
You can download mobile version from Android Play Store and Apple App Store.
Search Keyword will be ‘nod coco’ (all small letter)
For the PC version, you can simply go to our website www.nodcoco.com to register and use it.

What are your terms of service and privacy policy?

Please find our terms of service, here and privacy policy here. Any feedback on these, please contact us at support@nod.bizware.com

Can I upgrade my account during my trial?

Yes, you can upgrade your license at any time. If you signed up for the trial plan and it has not yet ended, you can upgrade instantly and enjoy all the features of a Standard and Premium plan. If you aren’t sure what kind of plan you signed up for, you can check the page ‘Features’, or send us an email at support@nod-bizware.com

How do I get a receipt for my purchase?

If you purchased NOD CoCo Standard or Premium from your mobile or PC, NOD CoCo will sends an email receipt for your purchase to the email address associated with your account. If you didn't receive an email receipt, please check your email spam or junk folder.

Story behind the app

NOD Biz story was started with an experience by Mr. Yun Seokgu,
CEO of NOD Biz. He had already renowned for his patent of Digital Right Management solutions in South Korea and participated in G20 Summit, Seoul, 2010 as a security service provider.

Then, he had been witness that government units and companies had put on enormous efforts and spending huge amount of money to protect confidential data, so the protection processes were extremely complicated,
but still couldn’t achieve highest safeguard.

From that experience, he decided to make one application so people can simply use it from any devices and can easily learn how to use, but has ultimate data protection.

It was how NOD CoCo’s came in to this world.
